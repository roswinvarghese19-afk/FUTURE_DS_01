# 📊 Sales Performance Dashboard (Excel)
📊 Interactive Excel Dashboard | Data Analytics Project | Business Insights
## 🔍 Project Overview
This project analyzes business sales data to uncover key insights related to revenue, profitability, customer behavior, and regional performance.

The dashboard is built in Microsoft Excel using Pivot Tables, Charts, and Slicers to create an interactive and client-ready analytics solution.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Identify top-performing products
- Analyze sales trends over time
- Evaluate regional and category performance
- Understand customer purchasing behavior
- Provide actionable business insights

---

## 📁 Dataset
- Source: Superstore Sales Dataset
- Includes:
  - Order Date
  - Sales
  - Profit
  - Quantity
  - Category
  - Region
  - Customer Name

---

## 🧩 Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel
  - Pivot Tables
  - Pivot Charts
  - Slicers
  - Calculated Fields
- Data Cleaning Techniques
- Basic Excel Formulas

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 🔹 KPI Metrics
- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin (%)
- Total Orders
- Average Order Value (AOV)
- Total Quantity Sold

---

### 🔹 Visualizations
- 📈 Sales Trend Over Time (Line Chart)
- 🏆 Top 10 Products (Bar Chart)
- 🥧 Sales by Category (Pie Chart)
- 📊 Profit by Region (Column Chart)
- 📊 AOV by Region

---

### 🔹 Interactivity
- Slicers for:
  - Region
  - Category
  - Segment

---

## 🧠 Key Insights

- Sales peak during November–December, indicating strong seasonal demand.
- A small group of top products contributes significantly to total revenue.
- The Technology category is the highest revenue generator.
- The West region shows the highest profitability and average order value.
- Customer behavior analysis shows repeat purchasing patterns.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Increase inventory and marketing efforts during peak seasons.
- Focus on high-performing products to maximize revenue.
- Expand product offerings in high-performing categories.
- Improve performance in low-profit regions through pricing or cost strategies.
